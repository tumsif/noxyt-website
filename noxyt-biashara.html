What is N-biashara ?

N-Biashara is a cloud based suite of features platform that allows you to quickly TURN ON the latest e-commerce features on your business while providing a future proof solution.

Features

  • Manage Sales And Stock Movement
  • Manage Customer Register
  • Accept Cash And Its Integrated To Mobile Money Payment Systems
  • Responsive Frontend. ie Works on all Smarphones, Tablets and PC's
  • Product Recommendations
  • NPS Analyser

Five Easy Steps

  1. Register Your Business Profile
  2. Add Product And Their Description
  3. Define Product Price
  4. Advertise on all media. eg Radio, Tv, Social Media
  5. Make Sales

Advantages

  • Easy Setup
  • Very Affordable
  • Increase Market Size And Brand Awareness With Online Merchandising
  • Give Your Whole Business An Online Presence
  • Track End To End Order Processing. ie From Order Capture To Delivery
