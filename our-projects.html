Meter Management System.

An MDMS provide application programming interfaces (API's) between the MDMS and the multiple destinations that rely on meter data. This is the first step to ensure that consistent processes and 'understanding' get applied to the data. Besides this common functionality, an advanced MDM may provide facility for remote connect / disconnect of meters, power status verification / power restoration verification and on demand read of remote meters.

Android App (Meter Management System).

Android App based on meter data include Smart meter deployment planning and management, Meter and network asset monitoring and management, Automated smart meter provisioning (i.e. addition, deletion and updating of meter information at utility and AMR side) and billing cutover, Meter-to-Cash system, workforce management system, asset management and other systems.

Fuel Card System.

In its infancy, fuel cards were only printed with the company name, vehicle registration and a signature strip on the reverse. No electronic data was stored. Fuelling sites would verify the company, vehicle registration (on the forecourt) against the card and also the signature written on the back. The site would allow access to the fuel once the retailer's receipt had been signed for and cross checked against the signature written on the back of the card.

Health and Safety Management System.

The Health and Safety Management System is a pioneering solution dedicated to centralizing and ensuring the accuracy of Health, Safety, and Wellness (HSW) data. It empowers users with real-time access to HSW information, facilitating prompt decision-making and proactive risk management. The system provides visibility into individual profiles, enabling targeted interventions and risk categorizations. Timely notifications prompt users to take necessary actions based on identified safety concerns. By democratizing HSW data, the system fosters a culture of transparency and collective responsibility. It also seeks to cultivate a culture of personal safety, instilling a sense of ownership and promoting safe behavior among individuals.
